About Me:

Hi, my name is Dylan! Coming from a Vietnamese refugee household, my parents instilled in me the values of hard work and humility. Growing up, I had no where near the amount of wealth and resources as everyone else; however, this put a chip on my shoulder. Being an underdog made me stronger, tougher, and more focused. I strive to be the living embodiment of my parent's values and will prove to everyone that hard work has no limit.
