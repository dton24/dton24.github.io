EDUCATION

I went to Evergreen Valley High School and was involved in multiple clubs on campus. Using data as a tool for decision making gave us an advantage over other clubs on campus. VSA and Interact became the two biggest clubs at EVHS, and the increase in membership made me fall in love with data. Soon, I realized that Data Analytics was the perfect profession for me.


Upon completing high school, I was faced with the significant decision of choosing a university. I decided on California State Polytechnic University - Pomona, a state school known for its community of relentless hard workers and underdogs. Cal Poly Pomona may not have the same breadth of resources that many private institutions boast, but it was a familiar scenario for me. I saw the limitations not as barriers, but as challenges that provided opportunities for growth, resilience, and innovation.
