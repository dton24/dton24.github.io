My Journey!

I went to Evergreen Valley High School, where I was actively involved in multiple clubs on campus. I frequently used Google Forms and Spreadsheets to collect and analyze data, helping my clubs with membership growth and retention. Little did I know, these experiences laid the foundations for my data analytics journey.


Upon completing high school, I decided on California State Polytechnic University - Pomona. I joined Cal Poly SWIFT, CPP MISSA, and CPP AKPSI, eventually becoming the Director of Data Analytics for CPP MISSA and the Vice President of Professional Development for AKPSI. Additionally, I founded the first ever student-led consulting group at CPP called PolyInsights, where our members do projects for real businesses.
